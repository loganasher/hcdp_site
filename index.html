HENDRICKS COUNTY DEMOCRATS WELCOMES YOU

Our mission is to help our county-wide and state-wide candidates get elected in a number of different positions. Each month we have a meeting with a special guest in order to inform Democrats across the county about what is happening in Democratic politics around the country.

We want to thank all Democrats who voted in the November 2018 election and the big showing we had. Your candidates did well and we will continue to work to #Turn Hendricks Blue and elect more Dems locally! Watch this space for updates from the local, state and national Democratic party as we have them.

Our meetings are held on the first Tuesday of each month at the Avon American Legion in Avon, Indiana from 6:00-8:30 pm. Dinner is available for purchase. Bring a guest and if you have questions please reach out to our officers or on Facebook! (No meetings in May or November during elections.)
